You MUST have a public Google Document with information to summarize.


The following are a list of steps to achieve this:

1. Open the text document, presentation, spreadsheet or drawing that you want to share with the public.
2. Click the “Share” button at the top right corner of the screen (under your email address) to open the Sharing Settings dialog box.
3. Click "Change…" in the "Who Has Access" section to open the "Sharing Settings Visibility Options" area.
4. Select “Public On The Web” and then click the field next to “Access: Anyone (No Sign-In Required).” Select the level of accessibility you are giving to the public from the pop-up menu: “Can Edit,” “Can Comment” or “Can View.”
5. Click “Save” to return to the general Sharing Settings dialog box, and then click “Done” to close the Sharing Settings box.
